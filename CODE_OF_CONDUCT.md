# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone — regardless of experience level, identity, or background.

## Standards

**Do:**
- Assume good faith.
- Be direct and specific. Prefer code, reproductions, and concrete examples over vague complaints.
- Critique ideas, not people.

**Don't:**
- Harass, insult, or belittle other contributors.
- Post sexualized, discriminatory, or threatening content.
- Share someone else's private information without consent.

## Enforcement

If someone's behavior is a problem, contact the maintainer by opening a private issue or by emailing the address on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Reports will be handled privately. Depending on severity, responses range from a private warning to a permanent ban from the repository.

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any project-adjacent communication.
